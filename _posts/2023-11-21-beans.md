# Welcome traveller!
Are you new to the world of Linux and unsure about which distribution (distro) to choose? With so many options available, it can be quite overwhelming. In this blog post I'll show you some of the best distros for beginners that cater to various needs and preferences. Either one of these distros I list will leave you well off.

# [Linux Mint](https://linuxmint.com/)
Linux mint is one of the most popular Linux distributions among new beginners due to its Windows 10 like interface, and extensive support. It offers a variety of preinstalled applications that cater to various needs such as office productivity, multimedia playback, web browsing, and more. The Linux Mint community provides excellent support through Reddit, forms, and Discord.

![cinnamon](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/2/2e/Linux_Mint_21_%22Vanessa%22_%28Cinnamon%29.png)

# [Fedora](https://fedoraproject.org/)
Fedora is a distro sponsored by Red Hat that focuses on providing the latest technologies and software updates to its users, while being stable at the same time. It's a little more challenging to use than Linux Mint but offers the best preformance for gaming, programming, and other workstation tasks. It also has excellent support.

![FEDORA](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:2000/1*KeTlKGwKtHDGawlv0JaYVg.png)

So in conclusion, If you're looking for a user-friendly interface similar to Windows 10 and require standard web browsing capabilities, Linux Mint is perfect for you. On the other hand, if you are a gamer or need to perform professional tasks, Fedora offers cutting-edge software updates and more. Additionally, Fedora is great if you have a newer laptop and are more comfortable with computers. No matter which distro you choose, remember that the Linux community is always ready to help and support new users in their journey.

# TLDR;

[Linux Mint](https://linuxmint.com/) - A user-friendly interface similar to Windows 10 with extensive documentation and preinstalled applications for office productivity, multimedia playback, web browsing, etc.

[Fedora](https://fedoraproject.org/) - Focuses on providing the latest technologies and software updates, great for gaming, programming, newer harderware, workstation tasks, etc. And offers excellent support.
